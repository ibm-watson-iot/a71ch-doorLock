Introduction

NXP A71CH Watson IoT product type is an A71CH device that has been trust provisioned by NXP with credentials (one EC key pair, one Device X509 certificate and one Gateway X509 certificate) supporting TLS access to Watson IoT Platform service. The NXP A71CH Enabled Door Lock application demonstrates:

  • How to provision an NXP A71CH Watson IoT product type device?
  • Connect the device securely with IBM Watson IoT Platform service.
  • Invoke remote control actions on the device.

Components

The application is composed of the following components.

Watson IoT Platform

Watson IoT Platform lets your apps communicate with and consume data from your connected devices, sensors, and gateways.

Node-RED

Node-RED is a tool for wiring together hardware devices, APIs, and online services in new and interesting ways.

A71CH Enabled Device

Use A71CH enabled device to connect to Watson IoT Platform and send device data.
